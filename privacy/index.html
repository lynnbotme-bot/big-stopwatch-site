Privacy Policy for Big Stopwatch

Effective date: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Big Stopwatch (“the App”) handles information. Big Stopwatch is a simple stopwatch utility and is designed to work locally on your device.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data.

The App does not require account creation and does not request personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

2. Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

Big Stopwatch does not use:

  • Third-party analytics SDKs
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Cross-app tracking technologies
  • Profiling for advertising or marketing

3. Data Sharing

Because the App does not collect personal data, it does not sell, share, or disclose personal data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage

Big Stopwatch runs locally on your device. Any runtime stopwatch state is used only for app functionality during use.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

6. Security

We design the App to minimize data handling. Since no personal data is collected by the App, exposure risk from app-side data collection is minimized.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and publish the revised policy at this URL.
